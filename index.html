🎀 Breast Cancer Awareness

🛡️ Prevention Tips

  • Maintain a healthy weight and BMI
  • Exercise regularly (30 minutes daily)
  • Limit alcohol consumption
  • Avoid smoking and tobacco products
  • Eat a balanced diet rich in fruits and vegetables
  • Limit hormone replacement therapy
  • Breastfeed if possible
  • Get regular health screenings

🔍 Self-Examination Guide

Step 1: Stand in front of a mirror with shoulders straight and arms on hips

Step 2: Look for changes in size, shape, or skin texture

Step 3: Raise arms overhead and check again

Step 4: Lie down and use finger pads to examine systematically

Step 5: Use circular motions covering entire breast and armpit

Step 6: Check for lumps while sitting or standing in shower

Frequency: Perform monthly, ideally 7-10 days after menstruation

⚠️ Warning Signs

  • New lump in breast or underarm area
  • Thickening or swelling of part of breast
  • Irritation or dimpling of breast skin
  • Redness or flaky skin around nipple
  • Nipple discharge (other than breast milk)
  • Changes in size or shape of breast
  • Pain in breast or nipple area
  • Inverted or retracted nipple
